Theme: Vintage Revival

Rediscover the charm and elegance of vintage fashion. Designers are invited to create modern interpretations of classic styles from past decades, celebrating timeless fashion trends.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Designs must be submitted in PDF format.
  • Each designer can submit up to three designs.
  • Designs should reflect the theme "Vintage Revival."
  • Entries should include a brief description of the era or style that inspired the design.
