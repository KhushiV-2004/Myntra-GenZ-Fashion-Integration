Theme: Eco-Fashion

Embrace sustainability and eco-friendly practices in fashion design. We invite designers to showcase their creations that prioritize environmental conservation and ethical fashion principles.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Designs must be submitted in PDF format.
  • Each designer can submit up to three designs.
  • Designs should reflect the theme "Eco-Fashion."
  • Entries should include a brief description of the sustainable practices used.
