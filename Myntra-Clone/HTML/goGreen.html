GoGreen Initiative

Our GoGreen initiative is aimed at promoting sustainability and protecting the environment. As part of this scheme, customers are encouraged to return the packaging material used for their purchases. This helps us to recycle and reuse the materials, significantly reducing waste and promoting a circular economy.

Environmental Impact

Did you know that packaging waste accounts for 40% of total plastic waste? By reusing and recycling packaging materials, we can make a significant impact on reducing environmental waste and promoting a greener planet.

At Myntra, we are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and promoting eco-friendly practices. Our GoGreen initiative is one step towards creating a sustainable future for everyone.

How It Works

  • Opt-in for the GoGreen scheme during your purchase by checking the GoGreen checkbox.
  • After receiving your order, collect the packaging material and ensure it is clean and undamaged.
  • Return the packaging material to us using one of the following options:
    • Hand it over to the delivery person on the day of delivery.
    • Our delivery person will come to collect the packaging within 2 days. You will be notified about the collection time, and the delivery person's phone number will be provided for you to arrange a suitable time.

Benefits

  • Contribute to reducing environmental waste.
  • Support a circular economy by promoting the reuse of materials.
  • Help us reduce the carbon footprint associated with packaging materials.

By participating in the GoGreen initiative, you are making a conscious choice to protect our planet. Thank you for your support!
