Theme: Tech and Fashion

Blend technology with fashion to create innovative and cutting-edge pieces that redefine the future of fashion. We invite designers to submit their designs that integrate technology in unique and stylish ways.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Designs must be submitted in PDF format.
  • Each designer can submit up to three designs.
  • Designs should reflect the theme "Tech and Fashion."
  • Entries should include a brief description of the inspiration behind the design.
